## About Me

I am currently a tenure-track assistant professor at the [School of Physical Science and Technology, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications](https://spst.bupt.edu.cn/). 
I received my Ph.D. degree from the School of [Systems Science, Beijing Normal University](https://sss.bnu.edu.cn/) in 2023, and completed postdoctoral research at the [Institute of Theoretical Physics, Chinese Academy of Sciences](https://itp.cas.cn/).
My research interests lie in statistical physics and machine learning, and I have published in Nature Communications, New Journal of Physics, and Physical Review E.
